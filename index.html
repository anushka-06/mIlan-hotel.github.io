About Us

Milan Hotel Group is a management and development company based in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains in Asheville, North Carolina.

Established in 2012, MHG has grown exponentially in a relatively short time, focusing on the discovery, conceptualization, and development of prudent investment opportunities and a high-end customer experience.
