Our Company Culture

Purposeful yet Conscientious

MHG aims to foster an engaging, productive, and rewarding work environment that respects and motivates its team members, allowing them to thrive and grow, both personally and professionally.

We operate on a specific set of core values: dedication, compassion, competence, and loyalty. We believe these traits reflect our business philosophy, from executives to staff, and we handpick our teams to embody them in everything we do. Following these ideals, we strive to demonstrate that successful companies are built by and for people, not profits. Despite the rapid growth and success of MHG, we remain dedicated to offering an extraordinary guest experience, rewarding opportunities for team members, and a lasting positive impact on local communities.

Scholarship

The M.U. Patel Memorial Scholarship was established to honor Manubhai U. Patel, an inspiring self-made businessman who was both well-respected and family-oriented. He always believed that the key to success in life was education, and our scholarship program seeks to promote this idea.

Each year, this scholarship will be given to a promising high school senior or college undergraduate who has a parent currently working with Milan Hotel Group. We encourage individuals who exhibit academic potential and community involvement to take our application essay!

*Applicants should provide documentation of the university, college, or trade school they will attend within one year of their application
