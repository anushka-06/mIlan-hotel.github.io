  • Jamie Tudico

    Sales & Marketing

    Jamie Tudico has a passion for Asheville, NC. She has spent the majority of her career traveling throughout the country, sharing the story of Asheville with customers and potential visitors alike. Originally from Tennessee, Jamie started her hospitality journey in catering sales. Upon relocating to Asheville in 2010, she grew her skill set as a national group sales manager for Omni Hotels. There she focused on selling not only the property but the destination of Asheville as a whole experience. As a former Director of Sales and Catering for Kessler, she brings a diverse portfolio of hotel experience to The Milan Hotel Group. Jamie is looking forward to representing MHG both in the Asheville community and beyond. In her free time, she enjoys travel as well as attending musical theatre and live concerts.   • Christopher Gasper

    Revenue Management

    Upon graduating from college, Chris took a relaxing summer to explore Mount Desert Island off the coast of Maine. He was first introduced to the hospitality industry while working as a front desk agent and shift manager at a beautiful oceanfront property in the vibrant town of Bar Harbor. For the past 15 years, Chris has been fortunate to continue his hospitality career in the thriving city of Asheville, North Carolina. While he’s been involved in many aspects of hotel operations he has a particular talent for his current position as Revenue Manager for Milan Hotel Group, where he focuses on developing strategies by utilizing in- depth market knowledge and segmentation, designing a specific course of action for each property. In his spare time, Chris enjoys volunteering for charitable causes and is an avid supporter of local music, art, and film.
